MY STORY

For over two decades, I have been part of the corporate world — building a career, taking on responsibilities, and growing through structured professional environments. Like many professionals, my identity was often defined by my designation, performance metrics, and organisational roles.

While these brought growth and stability, they also sparked a deeper curiosity within me — curiosity about people, journeys, decisions, and the human stories behind professional success.

This realization led to the creation of Beyond Title. This platform is my effort to explore meaningful conversations with professionals from diverse fields — to understand not just what they do, but who they are.

My goal is simple: To create conversations that inspire clarity, perspective, and growth. It is a journey of listening, learning, and sharing stories that truly matter.

MY PURPOSE

My goal is to create a platform where real conversations take place — conversations that go beyond designations and focus on human experiences, learning, and growth.
